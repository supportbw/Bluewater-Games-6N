💰 Per-Game Betting (Optional)

How it works:

• Bet on ANY individual match (€5, €10, €20, €50, €100)

• If you're the ONLY one who bets on that match, you get your money back

• If 2+ people bet on the same match, winner takes ALL!

Example:

• You bet €10 on France vs Ireland, predict France wins

• John bets €10, predicts Ireland wins

• If France wins → You get €20 total (your €10 + John's €10)

• If Ireland wins → John gets €20 total

🏉 How to Play

1. Enter your name to join

2. For each match, pick the winner (or draw)

3. Optionally check "I want to bet on this match" and enter amount

4. Submit before kick-off

5. Check "All Predictions" tab to see what others predicted!

⚡ Points System

Correct Winner Prediction 1 point

Example: If you correctly predict 10 out of 15 matches, you get 10 points total.
